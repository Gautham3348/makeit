Fruit-dessert

cooking time

preparation time :35 cook time:40

ingredients

  1. Assorted fresh fruits (such as strawberries, blueberries, raspberries, kiwi, pineapple, and grapes)

  2. 2 tablespoons honey or maple syrup (optional, for added sweetness)

  3. 1-2 tablespoons lemon juice or lime juice

  4. Fresh mint leaves for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  • Wash all the fruits thoroughly under cold water. Pat them dry with a paper towel or a clean kitchen towel.Prepare the fruits by slicing them into bite-sized pieces or chunks. You can slice the strawberries, cube the pineapple, halve the grapes, and peel and slice the kiwi. Mix all the fruits in a large bowl.

  • If you prefer a sweeter fruit salad, you can add honey or maple syrup to the fruits and mix well. Adjust the sweetness according to your taste.

  • Squeeze fresh lemon or lime juice over the fruit salad and gently toss to combine. The citrus juice will add a refreshing tanginess and prevent the fruits from browning.

  • Let the fruit salad chill in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld.

  • Before serving, garnish the fruit salad with some fresh mint leaves for added freshness and aroma.
