-BURGER-


Cooking time


preparation time :35 cook time:45



INGREDIENTS


  1. -1 pound (450g) ground beef (80/20 lean/fat is a good ratio for flavor)

  2. -Salt and pepper to taste

  3. -1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce (optional, for added flavor)

  4. -1/2 teaspoon garlic powder (optional, for extra flavor)

  5. -4 hamburger buns

  6. -Cheese slices (optional, choose your favorite type)

  7. -Lettuce leaves

  8. -Sliced tomatoes

  9. -Sliced onions

  10. -Pickles

  11. -Ketchup, mustard, and mayonnaise (optional, based on preference)


INSTRUCTIONS


  • -Preheat your grill or a skillet over medium-high heat.

  • -In a bowl, gently combine the ground beef, salt, pepper, Worcestershire sauce, and garlic powder. Mix thoroughly, but try not to overwork the meat as this can make the burgers tough.

  • -Divide the mixture into four equal parts and shape them into patties slightly larger than the buns, as they will shrink slightly during cooking.

  • -Place the patties on the preheated grill or skillet and cook for about 4-5 minutes on each side for medium doneness. Adjust the time based on how well-done you prefer your burger.

  • -If you want cheese on your burger, place a slice on each patty during the last minute of cooking to let it melt.

  • -While the patties are cooking, lightly toast the hamburger buns on the grill or in a toaster..

  • -Add the baked chicken to the tomato gravy and mix well.

  • -Once the patties are done, assemble the burger by placing lettuce, tomato, onion, and pickles on the bottom half of the bun. Add the cooked patty with melted cheese, and top with ketchup, mustard, and/or mayonnaise.

  • -Place the top half of the bun over the fillings and serve immediately.

  • -Feel free to customize your burger with additional toppings or sauces to suit your taste. Enjoy your homemade burger!


NUTRIENTS

  • -Protein:Approximately 20-30 grams

  • -Fat: Approximately 20-30 grams

  • -Carbohydrates: Approximately 30-50 grams

  • -Fiber: Approximately 2-4 grams

  • -suger:Approximately 5-10 grams
