PIZZA


cooking time


preparation time :50 cook time:55

INGREDIENTS


  1. -2 ¼ teaspoons (1 packet) active dry yeast

  2. -1 ½ cups warm water

  3. -4 cups all-purpose flour

  4. -1 teaspoon salt

  5. - 2 tablespoons olive oil

  6. -1 ½ cups pizza sauce

  7. -2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

  8. -Desired toppings (such as pepperoni, bell peppers, onions, mushrooms, olives, or any other toppings of your choice)

  9. -Fresh basil leaves (optional)

  10. -Grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

INSTRUCTIONS


  • -In a large mixing bowl, dissolve the yeast in warm water and let it sit for 5-10 minutes until it becomes frothy.

  • -Add the flour, salt, and olive oil to the yeast mixture. Mix until a dough forms.

  • Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 5-7 minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic. Form the dough into a ball.

  • Place the dough in a lightly oiled bowl, cover it with a clean kitchen towel, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1-2 hours until it doubles in size.

  • Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C). If you have a pizza stone, place it in the oven while preheating.

  • -Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium heat.

  • -Punch down the risen dough and divide it into two equal portions for two medium-sized pizzas.
    Roll out each portion of the dough into a round or rectangular shape, depending on your preference.

  • -Place the rolled-out dough on a baking sheet or a pizza peel dusted with cornmeal. Spread a generous amount of pizza sauce over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
    Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese evenly over the sauce.
    Add your desired toppings on top of the cheese.

  • -If using a pizza stone, carefully slide the assembled pizza onto the preheated pizza stone in the oven.
    Otherwise, place the baking sheet with the pizza in the oven. Bake for 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbling and melted.

NUTRIENTS


  • -Calories: The calorie content of a pizza depends on its size and ingredients.
    On average, a slice of cheese pizza can contain around 200-300 calories.

  • -Carbohydrates: Pizza crust is primarily made of carbohydrates.
    The carbohydrate content of a slice of pizza can range from 20-40 grams, depending on the crust type and toppings.

  • -Protein: The protein content of a slice of pizza can range from 8-15 grams, depending on the type and amount of cheese and meat toppings.

  • -Fat: Cheese and meat toppings contribute to the fat content of a pizza.
    A slice of pizza can contain around 10-20 grams of fat, depending on the toppings and cheese used.
