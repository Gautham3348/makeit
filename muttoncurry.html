Mutton-curry


Cooking time


preparation time :45 cook time:50

Ingredients


  1. -1 kg mutton, cut into pieces

  2. -3tablespoons oil or ghee

  3. -2 onions, finely chopped

  4. -2 tomatoes, pureed

  5. -1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste

  6. -1 teaspoon cumin seeds

  7. -1 cinnamon stick

  8. -2-3 green cardamom pods

  9. -1 teaspoon turmeric powder

  10. -1 teaspoon turmeric powder

  11. -2 teaspoons coriander powder

  12. -1 teaspoon garam masala

  13. -Salt to taste

  14. -Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

  15. -Water as needed

Instructions


  • -Heat oil or ghee in a large pot or pressure cooker.Add cumin seeds, cinnamon stick, and green cardamom pods.Sauté for a minute until fragrant.

  • -Add the chopped onions and sauté until they turn golden brown.

  • Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté until the raw smell disappears.

  • Stir in the tomato puree and cook until the oil separates from the mixture.

  • Add the mutton pieces, turmeric powder, red chili powder, coriander powder, garam masala, and salt. Mix well and cook for a few minutes to coat the mutton with the spices..

  • -Add enough water to cover the mutton, and pressure cook for 25-30 minutes or simmer on low heat until the mutton is tender and the flavors have melded.

  • -Once the mutton is cooked, garnish with fresh coriander leaves.

  • Serve hot with naan, rice, or any Indian bread of your choice.

  • Feel free to adjust the spices and consistency of the curry according to your taste preferences. Enjoy your flavorful homemade mutton curry!
