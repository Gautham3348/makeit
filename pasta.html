-PASTA-


Cooking time


preparation time :25 cook time:35



INGREDIENTS


  1. -8 ounces (about 225g) of your preferred pasta (spaghetti, fettuccine, penne, etc.)

  2. -4-6 quarts (3.8-5.7 liters) of water

  3. -1-2 tablespoons of salt (for the pasta water)

  4. -Sauce of your choice (marinara, Alfredo, pesto, etc.)

  5. -Optional toppings: grated cheese, fresh herbs, red pepper flakes, etc.

INSTRUCTIONS


  • -Bring a large pot of water to a rolling boil. Add 1-2 tablespoons of salt to the water. The salt adds flavor to the pasta..

  • -Add the pasta to the boiling water. Stir the pasta occasionally to prevent it from sticking together. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions or until it is al dente, which means itis cooked through but still slightly firm to the bite.

  • -The pasta is cooking, prepare the sauce in a separate pan if it requires cooking. If it's a ready-made sauce, you can simply warm it up in a pan.

  • -Once the pasta is cooked, use a colander to drain it. Be sure to reserve a small amount of the pasta water if you plan to use it to adjust the consistency of your sauce.

  • -Add the drained pasta to the sauce and toss it together. If the sauce is too thick, you can add some of the reserved pasta water to loosen it up.

  • - Serve the pasta in individual bowls or plates. Add any desired toppings such as grated cheese, fresh herbs, or red pepper flakes.

  • -Feel free to experiment with different sauces, ingredients, and flavors to create your perfect pasta dish. Enjoy your homemade pasta!.

NUTRIENTS


  • - Calories:Approximately 200 calories

  • -Protein: Approximately 7-8 grams

  • -Fat:Approximately 1 gram

  • -Carbohydrates: Approximately 40-45 grams

  • - Fiber:Approximately 2-3 grams
