FRIED FISH



cooking time


preparation time :50 cook time:55




INGREDIENTS



  1. - 4 pieces of fish fillets (such as tilapia, cod, or any white fish)

  2. - 1 cup all-purpose flour

  3. -1 teaspoon paprika

  4. -1/2 teaspoon garlic powder

  5. -1/2 teaspoon onion powder

  6. -1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional, for some heat)

  7. -Salt and pepper to taste

  8. -2 eggs, beaten

  9. -1/4 cup milk

  10. -Vegetable oil for frying

  11. -Lemon wedges for serving

  12. -Fresh parsley or cilantro for garnish


INSTUCTIONS


  • -Pat the fish fillets dry with a paper towel and season with salt and pepper.

  • -In a shallow dish, mix the flour, paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, cayenne pepper (if using), salt, and pepper.

  • -In another shallow dish, whisk together the eggs and milk.

  • -Dip each fish fillet into the flour mixture, coating both sides evenly. Shake off any excess flour.

  • -Dip the floured fillets into the egg mixture, ensuring they are coated thoroughly.

  • -Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium heat.

  • -Carefully place the coated fish fillets in the hot oil and fry for about 3-4 minutes on each side, or until they are golden brown and crispy.Once fried, remove the fish from the oil and place them on a plate lined with a paper towel to drain any excess oil.Serve the fried fish hot with lemon wedges, and garnish with fresh parsley or cilantro.

NUTRIENTS


  • -Calories: 200-250 kcal

  • -Protein: 15-20 grams

  • -Fat: 10-15 grams

  • -Saturated fat: 2-3 grams

  • -Carbohydrates: 5-10 grams

  • -Sodium: 300-400 milligrams
