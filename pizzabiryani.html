PIZZA-BIRYANI


Cooking time


preparation time :50 cook time:40



INGREDIENTS


  1. - 2 cups basmati rice, soaked for 30 minutes and drained

  2. - Whole spices (cinnamon, cardamom, cloves)

  3. - Sliced onions

  4. - Saffron strands soaked in milk

  5. - Ghee or oil

  6. - Salt to taste

  7. - Pizza sauce or tomato sauce

  8. - Grated mozzarella cheese

  9. - Sliced bell peppers

  10. - Sliced onions

  11. - Sliced mushrooms

  12. - Sliced olives

  13. - Sliced tomatoes

  14. - Pizza seasoning or mixed dried herbs

INSTRUCTIONS


  • - Begin by cooking the basmati rice with whole spices, sliced onions, ghee or oil, and salt in a traditional biryani style. You can add your choice of biryani spices such as cumin, coriander, and garam masala for added flavor.

  • - While the rice is cooking, preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).

  • - In a separate bowl, mix the pizza sauce with the cooked biryani rice.

  • - Take a baking dish and spread a layer of the sauced biryani rice evenly.

  • - Add a layer of the pizza toppings such as bell peppers, onions, mushrooms, olives, and tomatoes.

  • - Sprinkle grated mozzarella cheese over the toppings and add a dash of pizza seasoning or mixed dried herbs for extra flavor.

  • - Repeat the layering process, ending with a final layer of cheese on top.

  • - Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for about 20-25 minutes or until the cheese has melted and the biryani is heated through.

  • - Once done, remove the pizza biryani from the oven and let it cool slightly before serving.

  • - This fusion dish combines the aromatic flavors of biryani with the cheesy goodness of a pizza, resulting in a unique and delicious meal. Feel free to adjust the toppings and seasoning according to your taste preferences.

NUTRIENTS


  • - Calories: Approximately 300-400 calories per serving (1-1.5 cups)

  • - Protein: Approximately 10-15 grams

  • - Fat: Approximately 10-15 grams

  • - Carbohydrates: Approximately 40-50 grams

  • - Fiber: Approximately 2-4 grams

  • - Sodium: Varies depending on the amount of salt and cheese used
