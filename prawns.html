PRWANS BIRYANI


cooking time


preparation time :50 cook time:55



INGREDIENTS


  1. -500 grams of large prawns, cleaned and deveined

  2. - 1 teaspoon red chili powder

  3. - 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder

  4. -1 teaspoon biryani masala

  5. -Salt to taste

  6. -1 tablespoon lemon juice

  7. -2 cups basmati rice, soaked for 30 minutes and drained

  8. -4 cups water

  9. -1 bay leaf

  10. -2-3 green cardamom pods

  11. -3 tablespoons oil or ghee

  12. -2 onions, thinly sliced

  13. -1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste

  14. -1/2 cup plain yogurt

  15. -A pinch of saffron soaked in 2 tablespoons of warm milk

INSTRUCTIONS


  • -Marinate the prawns with red chili powder, turmeric powder, biryani masala, salt, and lemon juice. Let it sit for 30 minutes.In a large pot, bring water to a boil. Add soaked rice, bay leaf, green cardamom pods, cinnamon stick, and salt. Cook the rice until it is 70% done. Drain the rice and set it aside.

  • -In a separate pan, heat oil or ghee. Add the sliced onions and saut√© until they turn golden brown. Remove half of the fried onions and set them aside for garnish.

  • -Add chopped tomatoes and ginger-garlic paste to the remaining onions. Cook until the tomatoes are soft and the oil separates.

  • - Add marinated prawns and cook for a few minutes until they are almost done.Layer half of the partially cooked rice over the prawn mixture.Sprinkle half of the biryani masala, red chili powder, saffron milk, fried onions, coriander leaves, and mint leaves.

  • -Add the remaining rice and repeat the layering process with the rest of the ingredients.

  • -Drizzle some ghee on top and cover the pan with a tight-fitting lid. Cook on low heat for 15-20 minutes or until the rice is fully cooked and the prawns are tender.

  • -Once done, gently mix the layers before serving. Serve hot with raita or a side salad.Enjoy the aromatic and flavorful prawn biryani!
