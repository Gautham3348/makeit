-CHOCOLATE MILK SHAKE-


Cooking time


preparation time :25 cook time:20

INGREDIENTS


  1. -2 cups of vanilla ice cream

  2. - 1/2 cup of milk (adjust the amount based on your preferred thickness)

  3. -3 tablespoons of chocolate syrup or cocoa powder

  4. -Whipped cream (optional, for garnish)

  5. -Chocolate shavings or sprinkles (optional, for garnish)

  6. -Maraschino cherry (optional, for garnish)

INSTRUCTIONS


  • -Take a blender and add the vanilla ice cream, milk, and chocolate syrup or cocoa powder.

  • -Blend the mixture on high speed until it's smooth and creamy. If the milkshake is too thick, you can add a little more milk and blend again.

  • -Taste the milkshake and adjust the sweetness or chocolate flavor by adding more chocolate syrup or cocoa powder if desired.

  • -Once the milkshake reaches your desired consistency and taste, pour it into a tall glass.

  • If you like, you can top the milkshake with whipped cream and garnish with chocolate shavings or sprinkles.

  • -For an extra flourish, you can add a maraschino cherry on top.

  • Serve the chocolate milkshake immediately with a straw and a long spoon for enjoying both the shake and any remaining bits of ice cream.

  • -Feel free to get creative and add other ingredients such as chocolate chips, crushed cookies, or a scoop of peanut butter to personalize your milkshake. Enjoy your homemade chocolate milkshake!

NUTRIENTS


  • -Calories:Approximately 300-500 kcal

  • -Protein:Approximately 5-10 grams

  • -Fat:Approximately 10-20 grams

  • -Carbohydrates:Approximately 40-60 grams

  • -Fiber: Varies, but generally low

  • -Sugar: Approximately 30-50 grams
