-HOT DOG-


cooking time


preparation time :35 cook time:40



INGREDIENS


  1. -Hot dog sausages

  2. - Hot dog buns

  3. -Mustard

  4. -Ketchup

  5. - Pickle relish (optional)

  6. -Chopped onions (optional)

  7. -Shredded cheese (optional)

  8. -Jalapeno slices (optional)

INSTRUCTIONS


  • -Grill or heat the hot dog sausages on a stovetop or grill until they are fully cooked and slightly browned.

  • -Warm the hot dog buns by placing them on the grill for a minute or two, or you can toast them in a toaster oven.

  • -Place the cooked hot dog sausages inside the warmed buns.

  • -Add mustard, ketchup, and any other desired condiments such as pickle relish, chopped onions, shredded cheese, or jalapeno slices.

  • - Serve the hot dogs immediately with additional sides such as French fries, potato chips, or coleslaw.

  • -Feel free to customize your hot dog with your preferred toppings and condiments to create your perfect combination. Enjoy your homemade hot dogs!

NUTRIENTS


  • -Calories: Approximately 100-150 calories per hot dog

  • -Fat: Approximately 8-12 grams

  • -Carbohydrates: Approximately 1-2 grams

  • -Protein: Approximately 5-7 grams

  • -Sodium: Varies widely depending on the brand and type, but it can be high
