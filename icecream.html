Ice cream

Cooking time

preparation time :30 cook time:20

Ingredients

  1. 2 cups heavy cream

  2. 1 can (14 ounces) sweetened condensed milk

  3. 2 teaspoons vanilla extract

  4. Optional mix-ins such as chocolate chips, fruit, or nuts

Instructions

  • In a large bowl, whip the heavy cream with a hand mixer or a stand mixer until stiff peaks form.

  • Gently fold in the sweetened condensed milk and vanilla extract until fully combined.

  • If you want to add any mix-ins, gently fold them into the mixture at this stage.

  • Pour the mixture into a freezer-safe container and smooth the top with a spatula.

  • Cover the container with plastic wrap or a lid to prevent ice crystals from forming on the surface.

  • Place the container in the freezer for at least 6 hours or until the ice cream is firm.

  • Once the ice cream is frozen, scoop it into bowls or cones and enjoy.

  • Feel free to experiment with different flavors and mix-ins to create your own unique homemade ice cream.
