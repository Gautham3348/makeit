-LASANGA-


Cooking time


preparation time :35 cook time:45



INGREDIENTS


  1. -1 pound (450g) ground beef or Italian sausage

  2. -1 onion, finely choppedr

  3. -3 cloves garlic, minced

  4. -1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes

  5. -1 can (6 ounces) tomato paste

  6. -1 teaspoon dried basil

  7. -1 teaspoon dried oregano

  8. -Salt and pepper to taste

  9. -For the cheese filling

  10. -2 cups ricotta cheese

  11. -1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

  12. -1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

  13. -1 egg

  14. -2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped

  15. -salt and pepper to taste

OTHER INSTRUCTIONS


  • -Lasagna noodles, either oven-ready or pre-boiled according to package instructions

  • -Additional shredded mozzarella and Parmesan cheese for layering

INSTRUCTIONS


  • -Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  • -In a large skillet, cook the ground beef or Italian sausage over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and cook until the meat is browned and the onion is translucent.

  • -Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, dried basil, dried oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer the sauce for 20-30 minutes, stirring occasionally.

  • -In a medium bowl, combine the ricotta cheese, shredded mozzarella cheese, grated Parmesan cheese, egg, chopped parsley, salt, and pepper. Mix well to make the cheese filling.

  • -In a baking dish, spread a layer of the meat sauce on the bottom. Add a layer of lasagna noodles on top of the sauce.

  • -Spread a layer of the cheese filling over the noodles. Repeat the layering process with the remaining sauce, noodles, and cheese filling, ending with a layer of cheese on top.

  • -Sprinkle additional mozzarella and Parmesan cheese on the top layer.

  • Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes. Then, remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes or until the cheese is golden and bubbly.

  • -Allow the lasagna to rest for 10-15 minutes before slicing and serving.

  • Enjoy your homemade classic meat lasagna!

NUTRIENTS


  • -Calories:Approximately 300-400 calories per serving

  • -Protein:Approximately 15-20 grams

  • -Fat:Approximately 15-20 grams

  • -Carbohydrates: Approximately 25-35 grams

  • -Fiber:Approximately 2-4 grams

  • -Sugar: Approximately 5-8 grams
