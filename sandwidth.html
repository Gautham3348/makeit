sandwich


cooking time


preparation time :30 cook time:35

ingredients


  1. -2 slices of bread (any type you prefer, such as whole wheat, white, or sourdough)

  2. -Your choice of fillings (e.g., deli meats, cheese, vegetables, spreads)

  3. -Optional condiments (e.g., mustard, mayonnaise, pesto, hummus)

Instructions


  • -Lay the two slices of bread on a clean surface.

  • -Spread your desired condiments on one or both slices of bread.

  • Arrange your chosen fillings on one slice of bread. You can layer ingredients such as lettuce, tomato, cheese, meats, and any other toppings you prefer.

  • -Place the other slice of bread on top to create a sandwich.

  • If you want, you can cut the sandwich diagonally or straight down the middle to make it easier to eat.

  • Serve the sandwich with a side of chips, salad, or any other side dish you prefer.

  • Feel free to customize your sandwich with various ingredients and condiments to suit your taste preferences. Enjoy your homemade sandwich!

  • -Add the baked chicken to the tomato gravy and mix well.

  • Pour in the heavy cream and simmer for a few minutes until the sauce thickens.
