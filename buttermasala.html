BUTTER CHICKEN

cooking time

preparation time :30 cook time:35

ingredients

  1. pound (450g) boneless, skinless chicken thighs or breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces

  2. 1/2 cup plain yogurt

  3. 1 tablespoon lemon juice

  4. 1 teaspoon garam masala

  5. 1 teaspoon chili powder

  6. 1 teaspoon ground turmeric

  7. 1 teaspoon ground cumin

  8. 1 teaspoon ground coriander

  9. 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste

  10. Salt to taste

directions

  • In a bowl, mix all the ingredients listed under the chicken marinade.

  • Add the chicken pieces and marinate for at least 30 minutes or preferably a few hours.

  • Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).

  • Place the marinated chicken on a baking sheet and bake for 20-25 minutes or until the chicken is cooked through.

  • In a separate pan, melt butter or ghee over medium heat. Add the chopped onions and sauté until they turn golden brown.

  • ginger-garlic paste and sauté for a few minutes until the raw smell disappears.

  • Stir in the tomato puree, garam masala, and chili powder. Cook until the oil separates from the mixture.

  • Add the baked chicken to the tomato gravy and mix well.

  • Pour in the heavy cream and simmer for a few minutes until the sauce thickens.

  • Add dried fenugreek leaves and salt to taste. Adjust the seasoning as needed.

  • Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve hot with naan, rice, or any Indian bread of your choice.
